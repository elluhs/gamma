Welcome

Since 1990, the Glorious Gamma Chapter has been committed to breaking barriers in Philadelphia and beyond. As the first chapter established at an Ivy League institution, we recognize the importance of creating a voice for not only Latinas, but for all who come from marginalized communities—and we actively work to do so while embodying Sincerity, Loyalty, and Unity.
